Welcome to the Flower Shoppe

The biggest reason we gift flowers is to display an emotion. Whether its love, joy, affection, appreciation, sympathy, romance or apologies; flower giving communicates deep feelings in a simple but elegant manner.

Types of flowers

Roses are used to symbolise love, passion and romance

Sunflowers represent innocence, purity and joy

Orchids represent beauty, grace, and wealth

Lily's can symbolise death or beauty and resurrection.

"Flowers always make people better, happier and more helpful; they are sunshine, food and medicine for the soul."

-Luther Burbank
